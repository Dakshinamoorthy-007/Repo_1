Welcome to Chennai Corporation's Volunteer Registration Portal

About the Volunteer's Registration Programme

The Chennai Corporation Volunteer Registration Portal is an initiative to bring together enthusiastic citizens, students, and professionals who wish to contribute to the city’s development and welfare. This platform enables individuals to register as volunteers and actively participate in various civic activities, awareness drives, social outreach programmes, disaster response, and community welfare projects initiated by the Greater Chennai Corporation. Whether you're passionate about environmental efforts, public health, education, or urban planning, this portal provides an accessible way to get involved and make a meaningful impact.Volunteers will receive certificates of participation and, in some cases, the opportunity to take part in internship programmes or field projects . By registering here, you become a part of Chennai’s collective effort toward sustainable growth, inclusive governance, and citizen-led change.
Join hands with us to build a cleaner, healthier, and more resilient Chennai.

Eligibility Criteria

    Minimum Age: 16 years and above. (Students under 18 may require parental consent)
    Educational Qualification:
  1. Open to school students (Class 11 & 12)
  2. College students, graduates, and working professionals
    Residency:
  1. Must be a resident of Chennai or studying/working in Chennai
  2. Proof of address or institution may be required
    Skills/Interest Areas (optional but preferred):
  1. Public health
  2. Education
  3. Environmental initiatives
  4. Disaster response
  5. Community service
    Availability:
  1. Willingness to commit at least 4–6 hours per week (flexible based on project)
  2. Should be available for fieldwork or virtual tasks, as per assigned role
    Code of Conduct Agreement:
  1. Must agree to follow guidelines and code of conduct set by the Greater Chennai Corporation.
